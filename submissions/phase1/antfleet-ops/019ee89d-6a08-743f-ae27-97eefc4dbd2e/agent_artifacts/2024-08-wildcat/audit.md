# Audit: 2024-08-wildcat

## FixedTermLoanHooks fixed-term check blocks sanctioned-account escrow (`nukeFromOrbit` DoS)
- Location: src/access/FixedTermLoanHooks.sol : `onQueueWithdrawal` (interacting with src/market/WildcatMarket.sol : `_blockAccount` / src/market/WildcatMarketConfig.sol : `nukeFromOrbit`)
- Mechanism: `nukeFromOrbit` is the protocol's mechanism for forcibly removing an OFAC‑sanctioned lender — it calls `_blockAccount`, which (when the account has a balance) calls `_queueWithdrawal`, which unconditionally invokes `hooks.onQueueWithdrawal`. In `FixedTermLoanHooks.onQueueWithdrawal` the very first logic after the `isHooked` check is `if (market.fixedTermEndTime > block.timestamp) revert WithdrawBeforeTermEnd();`. Because `onQueueWithdrawal` is a *required* hook for this template (`requiredFlags` sets `Bit_Enabled_QueueWithdrawal`), there is no path that skips it. The fixed term can be set up to `MaximumLoanTerm = 365 days` and can only be *reduced* (`setFixedTermEndTime` reverts on increase), so for the duration of the term any call to `nukeFromOrbit(sanctionedLender)` reverts.
- Impact: A lender who becomes sanctioned mid-term cannot be blocked or have their assets moved to the sanctions escrow until the fixed term expires (up to a year), defeating the protocol's Chainalysis/escrow compliance guarantee and forcing the market to keep transacting with sanctioned funds.

## Deposit credits the requested amount rather than the amount actually received
- Location: src/market/WildcatMarket.sol : `_depositUpTo`
- Mechanism: `scaledAmount` is computed from the caller-supplied `amount` (`state.scaleAmount(amount)`) and the lender is credited `account.scaledBalance += scaledAmount` / `state.scaledTotalSupply += scaledAmount`, while the asset is pulled with `asset.safeTransferFrom(msg.sender, address(this), amount)`. The contract never measures the actual balance delta. For a fee-on-transfer (or otherwise non‑1:1) underlying asset, the market receives fewer tokens than `amount` but mints scaled supply as if it received the full `amount`, so `scaledTotalSupply * scaleFactor` (tracked debt) permanently exceeds `totalAssets()`.
- Impact: With a fee-on-transfer/deflationary asset, every deposit silently under-collateralizes the market; the inflated share accounting means the last lenders to withdraw cannot be made whole, and the market is pushed toward permanent delinquency.
